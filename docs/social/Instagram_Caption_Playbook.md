# Instagram Caption Playbook

Reusable caption system for **Next Gen Pickleball Academy** Instagram
(`@nextgenpickleballacademy`). Goal: post-ready captions in under five minutes,
on-brand every time.

**Source of truth:** `BRAND_GUIDELINES.md` (COPYWRITING GUARDRAILS) and
`src/data/site.ts` (handles, tagline, boilerplate). If anything here ever
conflicts with the brand guide, the brand guide wins — update this file.

> Audience: parents of kids ages **6–16** in **Montgomery County, MD**.
> Register parents as: reassuring, transparent, progress-oriented.

---

## The 30-second formula

Every caption follows the same shape:

1. **Hook** — one scroll-stopping line (a result, a question, or a vivid image).
2. **Proof** — what's actually happening in the clip (reps, cues, rallies,
   pathway). Show the work, don't hype it.
3. **EASE nod** — tie it to Ethics / Attitude / Skills / Excellence when it
   lands naturally (don't force all four).
4. **Place + CTA** — "Youth pickleball in Montgomery County, MD" + **one**
   primary CTA. Default CTA is always **free evaluation → link in bio**.
5. **Signoff** — the tagline: *Better than yesterday — together.*
6. **Hashtags** — pull from the bank below (8–12, MoCo-first).

---

## Voice rules (from `BRAND_GUIDELINES.md`)

**Words we use:** pathway, progress, reps, cues, EASE, triangle, community,
better than yesterday, evaluate, growth, family, next gen.

**Words/things we ban:**
- Hype without proof, sarcasm, insider slang, jargon.
- **No hard prices.** Never quote $25/monthly. The only live price is the $20
  drop-in, and that lives on `/schedule`, not in social captions. Tease, don't
  quote.
- **Skill levels are Red / Orange / Green / Yellow** — never "Beginner / Pro"
  or other synonyms.
- **Yellow Ball is invite-only** — never a public "register for Yellow Ball"
  CTA. Route tournament-track interest to `/yellowball/inquiry`.
- No "under 6" / "we'll make an exception" — ages 6–16 is strict.

**CTA hierarchy:** one primary CTA per post.
- Primary (default): **Get a Free Evaluation** → "link in bio" (`/free-evaluation`).
- Secondary (use sparingly, never alongside the primary): View Schedule
  (`/schedule`), See the Pathway, Meet the Coaches.
